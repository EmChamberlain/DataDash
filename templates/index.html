Data Dash

A quick and easy way to explore test scores!

DataDash will give you information on:

  • Test Score Analysis
  • Visualizing data from test scores
  • Different options to curve scores

    • To upload a file:

    1. Create a csv file, in excel, google spreadsheets, etc.

    2. Create two columns, one labled "Name", and the other "Score". Make sure these are spelled correctly.

    3. Add the names and scores to your csv file.

    4. Upload and get instant statistic summaries!
